# 💻 Student Guide: Taking Assessments

This guide explains how to use the platform to complete your coding assessments.

## 1. Getting Started
- **Account:** You will receive your login credentials from your department. Self-registration is disabled.
- **Login:** Enter your email and the provided temporary password.
- **Support:** If you cannot log in, contact your faculty or exam coordinator immediately.

## 2. Accessing Assessments
- Click on **Assessments** in the navigation bar.
- You will see a list of upcoming, live, and ended assessments.
- Click on a live assessment to view its details (Duration, Rules, etc.).

## 3. Starting the Exam
- When you are ready, click **Start Assessment**.
- **Important:** Once you click start, your personal timer begins. It cannot be paused even if you close the browser.

## 4. Solving Problems
- You will be taken to the **IDE (Workspace)**.
- Select a problem from the sidebar.
- Choose your preferred **Programming Language** from the dropdown.
- **Run Code:** Use this to test your solution against sample test cases. You can run code as many times as you like.
- **Submit Code:** When you are confident, click Submit. The system will test your code against hidden test cases and save your progress.

## 5. Finishing the Assessment
- You can submit the entire assessment early by clicking the **Submit Assessment** button in the header.
- **Auto-Submission:** If your time expires, the system will automatically save your work and mark your attempt as finished. You don't need to worry about losing progress if you run out of time.

## 6. Reviewing Progress
- You can see your previous submissions under the **My Submissions** tab.
- Once the assessment is graded or completed, you can view your results and score.
